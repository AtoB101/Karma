# Karma Trademark and Brand Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-05

This policy governs use of the **Karma** name, logos, domain names, and brand
assets.

## 1) Scope

The following are protected brand assets:

- The word marks: `Karma`, `Karma Pay`, `Karma Protocol`
- Official logos and visual identity assets
- Official domain names and social handles

## 2) What is allowed without prior approval

You may, without separate trademark permission:

- Refer to this project factually (for example: "built on Karma")
- Link to the official repository and documentation
- Use nominative references in articles, audits, and integrations

You must not imply endorsement, partnership, or official status.

## 3) What requires written permission

You must obtain written permission for:

- Product or company names that are confusingly similar to Karma branding
- Use of Karma logos in commercial products, ads, launch pages, or exchange listings
- Any representation suggesting your deployment is the "official Karma" service

Contact: licensing@karmapay.cloud

## 4) Prohibited uses

The following are prohibited:

- Presenting forked or modified deployments as official Karma
- Using Karma branding for phishing, fraud, malware, scam campaigns, or unlawful activity
- Registering confusingly similar domains/handles intended to impersonate official channels

## 5) Forks and derivative projects

Forks are allowed under the applicable code license, but they:

- Must keep original copyright/license notices
- Must use clearly different names and logos
- Must include a visible statement that they are unofficial and independently operated

## 6) Reservation of rights

No trademark license is granted by source code licenses alone. All trademark
rights are reserved except as expressly stated in this policy.
