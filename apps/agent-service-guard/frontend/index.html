How Karma protects deals

Every protected order follows one state machine: funds, responsibility, evidence, disputes, settlement.

1

Create a protected order

Define scope, price, delivery standard, and confirmation window.

2

Buyer locks funds

Buyer locks the full amount to prove ability to pay.

3

Seller locks bond

Seller locks minimum performance bond for baseline recourse.

4

Evidence auto-captured

Requests, responses, execution logs, and delivery proofs are recorded.

5

Settle or freeze

Confirm to release funds; disputes freeze relevant amounts for resolution.
